Carter Angelo

Founder of Carter Angelo Art Gallery. Carter grew up in a family of art collectors, insilling in him a lifelong love for fine art.He takes pride in sharing his passion and knowlegde of art with his visitors.

Carter Angelo Art Gallery

Carter Angelo opened this gallery with the goal of providing a space for emerging artists to exhibit their art and gain recognition. He has a keen eye for spotting promising artists and helping them develop their careers. Despite his advances age, Carter remains dedicated to his mission, and is always on the lookout for new talent to showcase in his gallery.
